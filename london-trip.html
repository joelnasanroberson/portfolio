Case Study 01

Premium Launch: London Market for Actors

Members‑only studio, modest spend. Launched the highest‑priced program — an in‑person UK networking track for American actors — producing 90 enrollments and $247,500 revenue with a waitlist.

$100 IG pilot $200/mo SMS (throttled) Proof‑forward landing Owned‑channel GTM

Context & Goal

Organization: One on One / Next Level (members‑only community)

Market reality: Some UK agencies had been skeptical of American talent; credibility and proof mattered more than volume.

Objective: Position a premium, scarce‑seat offer to members, fill the cohort, and validate a repeatable, low‑waste launch model.

Constraints

  • Audience: Closed — members only.
  • Budget: $100 IG validation; $200/mo SMS credits.
  • Timing: Limited window with specific talent/partner availability.

Strategy

  • Positioning: Lead with credibility — “Serious access, real rooms.” Back with partner Q&As, reels, alumni outcomes.
  • Lifecycle: Sequenced emails + segmented SMS (credit‑aware), partner co‑marketing, phone outreach, in‑studio print.
  • Landing: Concise copy, real quotes/logos/clips, short application path.
  • Enablement: Staff training + talk tracks for in‑room selling.
  • Testing: Angles (Access to reps vs. UK pipeline), micro‑CTAs, SMS timing.

Execution Notes

  • Owned media drove the launch; IG spend only validated creative.
  • Partner interviews → short video + quote assets for emails/landing.
  • SMS segmentation + throttling + time‑of‑day to stay within credits.

Outcomes

  • 90 seats @ ~$2,750 → $247,500 revenue.
  • Top program of past two years; next‑cohort waitlist created.
  • Low messaging cost; respectful member experience.

Why It Worked

  • Credible proof matched to a trusted member audience.
  • Clarity and respect: succinct value, real outcomes, minimal friction.

What I’d Scale Next

  • Retargeting (Meta/YouTube) around winning page/creative.
  • Member referral micro‑program + 3‑email win‑back for waitlist.
  • Non‑member RSVP gate that still routes through membership.

Capabilities — Email/SMS automation • CRM segmentation • Light paid social • Copy & landing testing • Video/motion/photography • Team enablement
