Context & Goal

Organization: One on One / Next Level (members-only community)
Market reality: Some UK agencies had been skeptical of American talent; credibility and proof mattered more than volume.
Objective: Position a premium, scarce-seat offer to members, fill the cohort, and validate a repeatable, low-waste launch model.

Constraints

  • Audience: Closed—members only (no broad top-of-funnel buying).
  • Budget: $100 IG pilot to validate creative; $200/month SMS credits (strict pacing to avoid overage).
  • Timing: Limited application window with specific talent/partner availability.

Strategy

  • Positioning: Lead with credibility—“Serious access, real rooms.” Support with partner Q&As, faculty reels, and alumni outcomes.
  • Lifecycle engine: Sequenced email drips and segmented SMS (credit‑aware), partner co‑marketing, targeted phone outreach, in‑studio print moments.
  • Landing page: Concise copy, real quotes/logos/clips, short application path.
  • Team enablement: Staff training and talk tracks to confidently sell in‑room during programming.
  • Focused experimentation: A/B angles (“Access to reps” vs “UK booking pipeline”), micro‑CTA language, and SMS timing.

Execution Notes

  • Built a full‑stack GTM around owned media; IG spend used primarily for creative validation (thumb‑stop rate, angle tests).
  • Produced partner interview prompts; cut short video + quote assets for emails and landing.
  • Implemented SMS segmentation, throttling, and time‑of‑day delivery to stay within credits while preserving response quality.

Outcomes

  • 90 seats at ~$2,750 → $247,500 in revenue; cohort filled and waitlist created.
  • Top performer of the past two years; member experience stayed high (no over‑messaging or public hard sell).

Why It Worked

  • Matched credible proof to an audience that already trusted the brand (members).
  • Funnel emphasized clarity and respect: succinct value, real outcomes, minimal friction.

What I’d Scale Next

  • Retargeting (Meta/YouTube) around the winning page and creative.
  • Member referral mini‑program; 3‑email win‑back for waitlisted members.
  • Test a non‑member RSVP gate (information session) that still routes through a membership‑first path.
